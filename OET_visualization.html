BECAUSE shows causation and explains WHY something happened. Statement 2 gives the REASON for the hurt mentioned in Statement 1.

"The insult was very hurtful, because it concerned not himself but another, his daughter, whom he loved more than himself."

üí°Why THEREFORE is Correct

THEREFORE shows consequence and indicates a RESULT that follows from the previous statement. Statement 2 is the RESULT of Statement 1.

"The roads were flooded due to heavy rain; therefore, the schools were closed for the day."
